Post-Extraction Care Guide

First 24 Hours

  • Bite firmly on the gauze pad for 30–45 minutes to control bleeding.
  • Do not rinse, spit forcefully, or use a straw for 24 hours.
  • Apply an ice pack to the outside of your cheek for 10 minutes at a time.
  • Take prescribed pain medication as directed.
  • Eat soft foods — yogurt, soup, mashed potatoes. Avoid hot foods and drinks.

Days 2–7

  • Gently rinse with warm salt water (½ tsp salt in a glass of water) after meals.
  • Brush carefully, avoiding the extraction site.
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol — they delay healing.
  • Gradually return to normal diet as comfort allows.
Call us immediately if you experience severe pain, heavy bleeding, fever, or swelling that worsens after 3 days.
