Braces & Aligners Care Instructions

Daily Brushing

  • Brush after every meal — food easily gets trapped in braces.
  • Use a soft-bristle toothbrush and fluoride toothpaste.
  • Floss daily using a floss threader or interdental brush.
  • Rinse with an alcohol-free mouthwash.

Foods to Enjoy

  • Soft fruits, cooked vegetables, pasta, rice, eggs, yogurt, cheese

Foods to Avoid

  • Hard candy, nuts, popcorn, ice cubes, chewing gum
  • Sticky foods — caramel, toffee, gummy candy
  • Hard foods — raw carrots, apples (cut into small pieces instead)

If a Bracket Breaks

  • Contact us on WhatsApp or call 9835938221 as soon as possible.
  • Do not try to fix it yourself.
  • If a wire is poking, cover it with orthodontic wax until your visit.
